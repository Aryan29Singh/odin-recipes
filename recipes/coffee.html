Chilled Cold Coffee

Icy cold coffee is simply the best beverage to enjoy on a hot summer day. Even though I tend to be more of a tea drinker, I do love a good cup of coffee occasionally â€“ and this cafe-style blended coffee drink is my favorite!

Ingredients

  • Coffee Powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice Cubes

Directions

  1. Add 4 teaspoon instant coffee powder, 2 tablespoon sugar and 2 tablespoon warm milk in a small mixing bowl.
  2. Mix until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add 2 cups chilled milk, 1 tablespoon milk powder, 3-4 ice cubes, and the coffee mixture to the medium jar of a blender and blend for a minute.
  4. Pour the coffee into the prepared glasses. Add the froth on top using a spoon. Sprinkle some coffee powder on top. Serve chilled.
